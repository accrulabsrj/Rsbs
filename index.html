Leadership & Professional Credentials

The firm is managed by experienced two partners who are members of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI).

BS

CA Bharat Singhal

FCA | Partner

CA Bharat Singhal is a Fellow Chartered Accountant with a strong focus on Statutory Audits, Banking Sector Assurance, and Project Finance. He possesses extensive experience in providing audit, assurance, direct taxation and indirect taxation services to non-corporate clients. A key strength is his banking sector experience, having served as Statutory Branch Auditor of a nationalized bank (SBI) for four consecutive years (FY 2019-20 to FY 2022-23) and being presently empaneled as Statutory Bank Auditor with Punjab National Bank (PNB). He also provides specialized consultancy on project finance and availing government subsidies (RIPS, MLUPY, TUFF, PMEGP) to the growing textile industry in the region.

Core Services & Proven Track Record

Our service offerings are directly supported by the past performance and experience of our partners.

Banking & Financial Sector Assurance

  • Statutory Branch Audit: Served as Statutory Branch Auditor of a nationalized bank (SBI) for four consecutive years (FY 2019-20 to FY 2022-23). Currently empaneled as Statutory Bank Auditor with Punjab National Bank (PNB).
  • Stock & Concurrent Audits: Undertaken stock audit assignments involving verification of drawing power, primary security, and monitoring of working capital limits.
  • Co-operative Sector: Conducted Quarterly & Annual Audit of Co-operative Societies (credit and agricultural).

Taxation & Regulatory Compliance

  • GST Advisory & Audits: Expertise in GST implementation, registration, classification, return schedules (GSTR-1, 3B, 7, 9), and conducting GST audits and reconciliation. Provided representation in GST assessments and appeals.
  • Income Tax & Tax Audit: Comprehensive tax audit services under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act, 1961; including specialist audits for partnership firms, sole proprietorships, and corporations.
  • TDS Compliance: Preparation of Form 26Q(AS), deposits, and reconciliation.

Specialized Regional Advisory (Textile Focus)

  • Project Finance: Successfully assisted numerous textile units in securing project finance and working capital facilities from financial institutions.
  • Government Subsidies: Expert guidance on availing subsidies under Central and State government schemes, including RIPS (Revised Integrated Processing Development Scheme), MLUPY, TUFF, and PMEGP.

Internal Audit & Control Assurance

  • Internal Audit: Provided internal audit services to various entities including Textile Manufacturing Units, Municipal Council, Agro Units, and Colour Chemical Trading units. Scope covers process compliance, financial controls, and operational efficiency.
  • Ind AS Exposure: Assisted the Main Auditor in the audit of Standalone and Consolidated Financial Statements of Grasim Industries Limited (2016).
